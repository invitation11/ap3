How It All Began

Our Story

A tale written in the stars

✦ JANUARY 2020 ✦

The First Meeting

It was a chilly winter evening at a mutual friend's gathering in Mumbai. Arjun noticed Priya across the room and knew — this was someone extraordinary. They talked until midnight, losing track of time entirely.

✦ AUGUST 2020 ✦

The First Date

Over candlelit dinner in Bandra, laughter turned into long, soulful conversations. They discovered a shared love for sunsets, old Bollywood, and Rajasthani cuisine. Something magical was blooming.

✦ DECEMBER 2024 ✦

The Proposal

On a moonlit boat on Lake Pichola, with the City Palace glowing behind them and rose petals floating on water, Arjun got down on one knee. Through happy tears, Priya whispered — "Forever and always, yes."

✦ JUNE 2025 ✦

Forever Begins

And now we invite you — our dearest family and friends — to be part of this beautiful beginning. Come celebrate with us as two souls become one in the romantic city of Udaipur.
